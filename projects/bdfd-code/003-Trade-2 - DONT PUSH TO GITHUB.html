What is trade?

1. Buy: Buying involves purchasing items or services by paying the agreed-upon price to the seller.

2. Sell: Selling involves transferring ownership of an item or service from a seller to a buyer in return for payment.

Now, we will discuss about the basic of forex and barter and how to do it.

3. Forex: Forex (Foreign Exchange) is the market where participants buy and sell currencies, aiming to profit from changes in exchange rates.

4. Barter: Barter is the exchange of goods or services directly for other goods or services without using money.

Basic Barter Explanations

This probably the hardest trade system to make, either learn it or just copy the code, up to you. :)

Barter means trade things to things. So what you wanna change is the variable. For now, let's create another variable and call it as "drink".

For example, the price of 2x foods are 3x drinks. So, if you want to trade less than 2 foods or less than 3 drinks, the bot will block your action or if you trying to trade 3 foods or 4 drinks will do the same

That's what we call as "multiples of X". For example, multiples of 2 is 2, 4, 6, 8,.. and multiples of 3 is 3, 6, 9, 12,..


Save the trade rate you want into temporary variables. 

$c[Trade Rate]

Now, we just need to add the real calculation here.

What "real calculation" we need?

So, the amount we input in $message[3] is might not yet valid. Because, users could put any number's there. What I'm trying to do in this step is to know automatically when they try to put 99 of foods or drinks: What's the valid number should be. 

$var[validAmount;$divide[$var[amountYouGive];$var[DrinkToFood]]]
$var[amountYouGet;$multi[$var[validAmount];$var[FoodToDrink]]]

$title[Result]
$addField[Valid amount:;$var[validAmount] $var[itemYouGive](s)]
$addField[You get:;$var[amountYouGet] $var[itemYouAsk](s)]

2 foods could get 3 drinks, so if we put 99 foods, we should get 49*3 = 147 drinks. It's because: 99 divided by 2 is equal 49.5 and the valid number is 49 only.
